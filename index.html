What is GI-Synergy?

GI-Synergy is a multi-botanical antimicrobial capsule from Apex Energetics, a practitioner-only nutraceutical brand based in Lake Forest, California. The formula combines berberine-bearing botanicals (typically Berberis aristata or Coptis chinensis) with undecylenic acid (a fatty acid derived from castor oil with documented anti-candida activity), plus oregano leaf extract, garlic bulb extract, and a small stack of supporting bitter and carminative herbs. Apex positions GI-Synergy alongside its companion product H-PLR (a separate parasite-aware botanical) — many practitioners run them together in 30, 60, or 90 day cycles. The product is not a probiotic, does not contain live cultures, and is not intended to replace prescription antibiotics or antifungals. Apex sells exclusively through credentialed clinicians and ships sealed bottles with lot-traceable numbers.
